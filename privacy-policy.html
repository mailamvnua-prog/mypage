Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This application ("Mai LAm") is a personal tool used to upload and publish video content to TikTok via TikTok's official API, on behalf of its single authorized user.

What data this app accesses

  • TikTok OAuth access token and refresh token, used only to authenticate API requests.
  • Video files provided directly by the user for the purpose of publishing to TikTok.

What this app does NOT do

  • Does not collect data from any TikTok user other than the authorized account owner.
  • Does not sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties.
  • Does not use data for advertising or analytics purposes.

Data storage

Tokens and video files are stored locally on the user's own device and are not transmitted to any server other than TikTok's official API.

For questions, contact the app owner directly.
